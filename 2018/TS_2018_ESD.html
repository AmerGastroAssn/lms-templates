The learning curve of endoscopic submucosal dissection (ESD) is very steep due to its complexity. The development of an effective training program is key to acquiring proficiency. This is compounded by the limited number of U.S. institutions using ESD, the lack of devices and techniques that allow dissections to be completed safely and quickly.

This presentation is a recording from the 2018 AGA Technology Summit as presented on March 21, 2018.

Presentation: 

  • Training issues regarding ESD and advanced resection techniques (approx. 12 minutes)
    Hiroyuki Aihara, MD, Ph.D., FASGE

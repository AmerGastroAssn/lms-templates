These slides were presented as part of the 2018 Crohn’s and Colitis Congress pre-workshop on fecal microbiota transplantation (FMT). The slides cover the current state of evidence on the safety, efficacy and administration of FMT to treat IBD, C. difficile infection and other related conditions for both adult and pediatric populations. The current U.S. regulatory framework for FMT is also addressed.

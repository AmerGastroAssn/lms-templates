Regional Practice Skills Workshops (RPSW) 2016

AGA held one-day regional workshops for GI fellows and early-career GIs in late 2016. During these practice skills workshops, which are held throughout the U.S. and are open to members and non-members, senior and junior GI leaders guide you through the various practice options and address topics rarely discussed during fellowship.

Topics include employment models, partnerships, hospital politics, billing and coding, compliance, contracts, health care reform and the Medicare Access and CHIP Reauthorization Act of 2015 (MACRA), and more. These provide a great opportunity to develop an effective action plan for achieving your career goals and to ensure you're on track for success.

Get point-of-care GI guidelines at your fingertips. Download the AGA Clinical Guidelines App today.

Easily access AGA's gold-standard gastroenterology and hepatology guidelines and decision support tools. Input patient information to guide decision making at point-of-care.

  • Review snapshots of evidence-based care recommendations.
  • Input information in a step-by-step format and develop informed decisions using the high-quality evidence.
  • Take notes and bookmark information for future reference and quicker decision making.

The AGA Clinical Guidelines App includes the most recent AGA guidelines:

  • Hepatitis B Reactivation
  • Drug Therapy for Crohn's Disease
  • Constipation
  • IBS Drug Management
  • Colonoscopy After Polypectomy
  • Pancreatic Cysts

More guidelines are on their way. Keep your app up-to-date for the latest guidelines.

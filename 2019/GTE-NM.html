The Gastroenterology Teaching Project (GTP) was a continuing educational resource started in 1975 by the AGA Institute Education & Training Committee. For over 30 years, this project provided gastroenterologists, hepatologists, basics scientists and others with sets of images that were developed by experts in their respective fields.

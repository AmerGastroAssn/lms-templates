The HCV revolution: Are you and your practice ready?

The goals of this activity are to enhance clinical and practical knowledge and provide tools to offer patients with chronic hepatitis C virus (HCV) infection optimal care.

More information

Learning objectives
  • List the benefits of HCV treatment to the individual and society
  • Describe the barriers to engagement in HCV care
  • Apply strategies to foster engagement in HCV care

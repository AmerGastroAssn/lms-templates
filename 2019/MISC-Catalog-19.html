Description

Find exclusive AGA resources to supplement your training and help advance your career in the 2018-2019 GI Resource Catalog. Whether it’s the latest advances in GI patient care, discovering networking opportunities or navigating the future of medicine, AGA has you covered:

  • Test your knowledge and identify education gaps with educational tools like Digestive Diseases Self-Education Program® (DDSEP) 8 or the AGA Postgraduate Course Resources
  • Attend AGA conferences to remain current on novel research in the field and network with other professionals.
  • Utilize AGA’s practice management resources to enhance the efficiency and probability of your practice in an era of accountable care.

The GI Resource Catalog is organized by eight sections, making it easier for you to navigate to your interests:

  1. Education
  2. Clinical Practice
  3. Patient Resources
  4. Practice Management
  5. Research
  6. Publications
  7. AGA Online
  8. AGA Events

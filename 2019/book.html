Description

New for 2019, the printed abstract book outlines recent clinical advances that impact how care is delivered that were presented at the live course. Keep this on your bookshelf and reference it all year long.

  • Includes text-based abstracts, algorithms and take away points from presentations.

These summaries - written by the authors of the journal article - highlight the clinical application of the study, including practical take-home points for use in the clinical care setting.
  • Extra space for note taking.

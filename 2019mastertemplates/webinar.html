Description

Join AGA, the Endocrine Society and Clinical Care options for the last complimentary webinar in a five-part series designer to improve your competence in identifying and managing patients with or at risk for nonalcoholic steatohepatitis (NASH), and in learning about emerging NASH therapies.

Hot topics in NASH: Real-world practice implications of NASH trial designs
Faculty

Stephen A. Harrison, MD, COL(Ret.), FAASLD
Visiting Professor of Hepatology
University of Oxford
Oxford, United Kingdom

Learning objectives
  • Evaluate risk and apply appropriate methodology to characterize NAFLD and diagnose NASH in individual patients.
  • Apply best practices in the management of patients with NAFLD based on
    • evolving data on lifestyle modification and medical treatment.
  • Critically evaluate data on emerging therapies for NASH, including evidence of reversal of fibrosis, risks, and benefits, and their potential utility in the treatment of patients with NASH.
  • Demonstrate an understanding of clinical trial design and evaluation of endpoints in the setting of NAFLD/NASH.
